Analytics Modules

Module-based analysis built for action

Six focused modules covering KPI overview, placement performance, full event traceability, recurring failure patterns, period comparison, and reject hotspot mapping.

Summary

KPI cards for high-level visibility of reject totals, cost impact, and machine usage. Includes a corrective action monitor — select any component and track its spitout rate over time to verify if fixes are working.

Successful Placements

Analyse successfully placed components with placement quantities, board-level statistics, and component value. Includes spit-to-success ratio per component for a clear view of placement health.

Individual Spit Events

Full traceability for every reject and spit event — component description, PCB location, trolley and feeder slot, board name, MO, machine, date, timestamp, reject reason, and cost.

Repeated Locations

Identify recurring reject positions and repeated placement failures on the PCB to isolate persistent issues and support faster root-cause investigation.

Period Comparison

Compare two date and time periods side by side for any run. View spitout comparison, cost comparison, loss per board comparison, and overlaid spit timeline graphs for both periods.

Heatmap of Trolleys & Slots

Visual reject hotspot mapping across machine trolleys and feeder slots. Toggle between Reject Count and Cost Impact modes, hover for feeder number, slot number, spit count, reject cost, and top component details.

Reporting and Export

Export structured analytics in one step

Generate automated Excel reports with structured multi-sheet reporting, full analytics export functionality, and integrated Pareto chart analysis of rejection trends.
