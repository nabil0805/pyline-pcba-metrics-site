Overview of PCBA Metrics

PCBA (Printed Circuit Board Assembly) metrics are essential for evaluating the efficiency and quality of the production process. These metrics help in identifying areas for improvement and ensuring that the production meets the required standards.

Key Metrics Tracked

  • Yield Rate: The percentage of good units produced compared to the total units started.
  • Defect Rate: The number of defects found per unit produced, indicating quality control effectiveness.
  • Cycle Time: The total time taken to produce a unit, which impacts overall production efficiency.
  • Rework Rate: The percentage of units that require rework, highlighting areas needing attention.

Significance of PCBA Metrics

Tracking these metrics allows manufacturers to optimize their processes, reduce costs, and improve product quality. By leveraging data from PCBA metrics, companies can make informed decisions that enhance production efficiency and customer satisfaction.
