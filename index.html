How It Works

From raw logs to actionable insight

The complete data flow is automated, so production teams can focus on improvement instead of manual spreadsheet work.

01. Machine generates logs

SMT machines produce detailed production logs for each board including runtime, rejects, placements, and stop events.

02. Software scans and processes

Proline scans configured folders and processes log files in seconds without manual preparation or reformatting.

03. Data visualised and analysed

Interactive graphs, analytics modules, and filters present information in a clear format for operators and engineers.

04. Teams act faster

Downtime, reject trends, and high-value losses become visible immediately to drive targeted process action.
